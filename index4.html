Market shift

The old job search doesn't work like it used to.

Applying broadly used to be rational. Volume mattered more. Today recruiters scan faster, AI-generated resumes flood the market, and generic applications disappear. The issue is no longer just how many jobs you apply to. It is whether your signal matches the role quickly enough to get noticed.

It's not just a numbers game anymore. It's a signal game.

More resumes

Every opening attracts more volume, more repetition, and more lookalike applications than it used to.

Less recruiter attention

Decision-makers move fast. If the match is not clear quickly, the role moves on without you.

Generic applications disappear

A broad resume and vague positioning create motion, but often not enough signal to earn a serious look.

Behavior change

The product changes workflow, not just wording.

SignalBench is built to change what you do before you apply: which roles you choose, how you read the opportunity, and how you decide whether your background will land the way you think it will.
